KYC Help | Instructions

Welcome to the KYC Help Page. Here you can find information and instructions to help you with the KYC.

Document Instructions

KYC process requires Login

  • Provide your personal information in the "Personal Details" section of the form.

  • Your personal information requires you to upload your images (personal photographs, document images, etc.)

    Uploading Documents

    Follow these steps to upload your documents:

    1. Login to your account.
    2. Navigate to the KYC section.
    3. Click on the "Upload Documents" button.
    4. Select the documents you want to upload.
    5. Click "Submit" to complete the process.

    To complete the KYC process, you will need to provide the following documents:

    • Government-issued ID (e.g. citizenship proof, passport)

  • Provide information about your family in the "Family Details" section of the form.

  • Provide information about your address in the "Address Details" section of the form.

  • Provide information about your income in the "Income Details" section of the form.

Others

If you encounter any issues during the KYC process, here are some common solutions:

  • Make sure your documents are clear and legible.
  • Check that your uploaded files are in the correct format (PDF, JPEG, etc.).
  • If your documents are rejected, follow the instructions provided and resubmit.
